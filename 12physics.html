Year 12 Physics

This subject builds off everything you have learnt from Year 11 Science related to physics.

Its expected that you have done 1.4 (Year 11 Science) AND have basic physics knowledge.

You will learn about AND be assessed in the following:

INTERNALS

- 2.5 Demonstrate understanding of atomic and nuclear physics - 3 Credits

- 2.1 Carry out a practical physics investigation that leads to a non-linear mathematical relationship - 4 Credits

EXTERNALS

- 2.4 Demonstrate understanding of mechanics - 6 Credits

- 2.6 Demonstrate understanding of electricity and electromagnetism - 6 Credits

- 2.3 Demonstrate understanding of waves - 4 Credits

This subject is endorsed.

This course leads to Year 13 Physics.

ESA Study guide for Year 12 Physics is highly recommended.
