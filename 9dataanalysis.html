DATA ANALYSIS

Year 9 Data Analysis

Data analysis is all about taking measurements of data and analysing how it works.

You will learn this practically through investigations by collecting data.

An example can be investigating forces of motion and analysing the data.

But what is analysing?

Analysing in science means to investigate the relationships between data sets.

For example, investigating the relationship of distance and time, and how they relate to velocity.

This website does not directly teach this topic, it simply provides resources to help studying, your science teacher will teach it for you.
