Year 13 Biology

This subject will build off of everything you have learnt from Year 12 Biology.

It is expected that you already know Year 12 Biology before continuing.

INTERNALS

- 3.4 Demonstrate understanding of how an animal maintains a stable internal environment - 3 Credits

- 3.7 Demonstrate understanding of human manipulations of genetic transfer and its biological implications - 3 Credits

EXTERNALS

- 3.3 Demonstrate understanding of the responses of plants and animals to their external environment - 5 Credits

- 3.6 Demonstrate understanding of trends in human evolution - 4 Credits

This subject is endorsed & UE approved.

3.4 & 3.7 offers UE literacy credits for reading if you need it.

3.3 & 3.6 offers UE literacy credits for reading & writing if you need it.

A Level 3 Biology SciPad is highly recommended for Internal and External assessments.

ESA Study guide for Year 13 Biology is recommended BUT not needed.
