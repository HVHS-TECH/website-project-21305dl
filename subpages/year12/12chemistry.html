YEAR 12 CHEMISTRY

Year 12 Chemistry

This subject builds off everything you have learnt from Year 11 Science related to chemistry.

Its expected that you have done Year 11 Science AND have basic chemistry knowledge.

You will learn about AND be assessed in the following:

INTERNALS

- 2.7 Demonstrate understanding of oxidation-reduction - 3 Credits

- 2.1 Carry out a practical investigation into a substance present in a consumer product using quantitative analysis - 4 Credits

EXTERNALS

- 2.6 Demonstrate understanding of chemical reactivity - 4 Credits

- 2.5 Demonstrate understanding of the properties of selected organic compounds - 6 Credits

- 2.4 Demonstrate understanding of bonding, structure, properties and energy changes - 4 Credits

This subject is endorsed.

This course leads to Year 13 Chemistry.

A Level 2 Chemistry SciPad is highly recommended for Internal and External assessments.

ESA Study guide for Year 12 Chemistry is recommended BUT not needed.
