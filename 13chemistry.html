Year 13 Chemistry

This subject will build off of everything you have learnt from Year 12 Chemistry.

It is expected that you already know Year 12 Chemistry before continuing.

INTERNALS

- 3.7 Demonstrate understanding of oxidation-reduction processes - 3 Credits

- 3.2 Demonstrate understanding of spectroscopic data in chemistry - 3 Credits

- 3.3 Demonstrate understanding of chemical processes in the world around us - 3 Credits

EXTERNALS

- 3.5 Demonstrate understanding of the properties of organic compounds - 5 Credits

- 3.4 Demonstrate understanding of particles and thermochemical principles - 5 Credits

- 3.6 Demonstrate understanding of equilibrium principles in aqueous systems - 5 Credits

This subject is endorsed & UE approved.

3.3 offers UE literacy credits for reading & writing as a report internal if you need it.

A Level 3 Chemistry SciPad is highly recommended for Internal and External assessments.

ESA Study guide for Year 13 Chemistry is recommended BUT not needed.

HVHS gives the option to EITHER do 3.3 (Chemical processes) OR 3.6 (Equilibria)
