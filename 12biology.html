Year 12 Biology

This subject will build off of everything you have learnt from Year 11 Science related to biology.

It is expected that you already know Year 11 Science AND have basic biology knowledge.

INTERNALS

- 2.1 Carry out a practical investigation in a biology context, with supervision - 4 Credits

- 3.2 Integrate biological knowledge to develop an informed response to a socio-scientific issue - 3 Credits

EXTERNALS

- 2.5 Demonstrate understanding of genetic variation and change - 4 Credits

- 2.7 Demonstrate understanding of gene expression - 4 Credits

This subject is endorsed.

3.2 is offered to Year 12 Biology students, and is worth 3 UE literacy credits for reading.

2.5 offers UE literacy credits for writing.

A Level 2 Biology SciPad is highly recommended for Internal and External assessments.

ESA Study guide for Year 12 Biology is recommended BUT not needed.
